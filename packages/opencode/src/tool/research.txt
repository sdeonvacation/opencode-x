Deep research: Investigates a question through multi-source web research with fact-checking.

Performs autonomous multi-phase research:
1. Plans diverse search queries approaching the topic from different angles
2. Searches the web in parallel across all query angles
3. Reads and extracts content from top sources (budget-capped)
4. Extracts falsifiable factual claims from each source
5. Groups and deduplicates facts
6. Cross-checks each fact via adversarial jury (majority-reject kills a claim)
7. Synthesizes a cited report with certainty ratings

Use this tool when the user needs thorough, multi-source research with fact verification. Returns a structured report with citations, confidence ratings, and statistics about the research process.

The research process may take 1-3 minutes depending on the breadth of the question.
